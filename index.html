Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 27, 2026

Welcome to Omni Note. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to protecting your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

Omni Note is an offline-first application. All of your notes, lists, and application settings are stored locally entirely on your device. We do not have access to your content.

2. Third-Party Services

Our application does not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising services that would track your behavior or collect your personal information.

3. Data Security

Because your data remains exclusively on your device, its security inherently relies on the physical security of your local device. We recommend keeping your device secured.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the developer email provided on our Google Play Store listing.
